About
Sikika Road Safety.

At Sikika Road safety, we are dedicated to improving traffic safety and mobility so that motorists can arrive at their destinations faster and safer. Our high performance road promotions combine with innovative systems and services help bring the best roadway systems, sensitizations and services into reality. We have been your partners for over two years to continue to bring innovation to the transportation safety industry.

1 - OUR MISSION

Sikika Road safety mission is dedicated to reducing crashes, deaths and injuries on our national highways by promoting safe driving behaviors through creating public awareness and safety innovative systems implementations.

2 - OUR VISION

Sikika Road safety vision is to be recognized as the leading organization into road safety sensitization and intelligent solutions implementation to reduce risks on the roads.

3 - CORE VALUES

Our Hardwork, Dedication and Integrity is aligned towards encouraging all road users to take road safety seriously in order to reduce deaths, injuries, Children being left orphans, Dreams been cut short...

OUR COMPETITIVE STRATEGY IS:

  1. Ability to create effective road safety awareness.
  2. Ability to provide innovative systems e.g. USSD, Mobile web etc.

OUR APPROACH.
Sikika Road Safety Champions.

  1. We encourage drivers to watch on speed.
  2. We advise the motorists to avoid drunk driving.
  3. We encourage all drivers to avoid careless overtaking.
  4. We encourage the passengers to buckle up safety belts all times.
  5. Motorists are encouraged not to tamper with speed governors.
  6. Drivers are encouraged not to driver roadworthy vehicles and to take their vehicles for service regularly.
  7. We encourage drivers to avoid distracted driving e.g Use of mobile phones.
  8. We encourage all road users to obey traffic rules and regulations.
